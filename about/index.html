About AnubisX Framework

Version: 3.0.0 — Scientific Re-Architecture

Date: July 2026

License: CC BY 4.0

Project Status

The framework is presented as a research methodology with prototype feasibility evidence. The formal experiment catalog (38 experiments) and benchmark suite (24 benchmarks) are fully specified but unexecuted. Four of five behavioral modalities remain untested. No ground-truth labels are available, preventing computation of standard accuracy metrics.

Philosophy

This work advocates for scientific rigor in behavioral attribution through:

  • Axiomatic foundations — 16 logically necessary axioms
  • Mathematical specification — 292 defined objects across 24 categories
  • Pre-specified validation — 31 acceptance criteria across 4 tiers
  • Transparent limitation disclosure — All limitations documented

Open Science

The framework is released as open source under CC BY 4.0 with complete documentation. It is intended as a foundation for systematic research, not as a validated operational system.
