Biography

Ahmed Awad, known professionally as NullC0d3, is an independent researcher specializing in behavioral digital attribution, computational stylometry, and digital forensics. With a background in software engineering and cybersecurity, Awad identified critical gaps in the formal foundations of digital attribution methodologies and undertook a multi-year research program to develop a comprehensive, mathematically rigorous framework. The result is the AnubisX Framework — the first complete formal system for behavioral digital attribution, encompassing axiomatic foundations, a formal theory of behavioral identity, a comprehensive mathematical framework, a systematic algorithm catalog, a six-layer architecture, and a pre-specified validation infrastructure. Awad's work is conducted under the principles of open science, with all framework components, source code, experimental data, and limitations publicly documented and freely available.
